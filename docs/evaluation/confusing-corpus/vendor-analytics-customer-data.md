# Vendor Analytics Customer Data Sharing Standard

## Purpose

This standard controls sharing customer data with third-party analytics vendors for reporting, forecasting, and product usage analysis.

## Policy

Vendors may receive customer data for analytics only when the data is aggregated or de-identified before transfer. Vendors must not receive raw production customer records unless legal approval and privacy approval are recorded.

Vendor analytics transfers require a data processing agreement, privacy impact assessment, and transfer inventory entry. Vendor access must be reviewed every ninety days.

This standard does not permit contractors to access production systems and does not permit sandbox copies of raw production customer data.

## Evidence

Required evidence includes data processing agreement, privacy impact assessment, transfer inventory entry, de-identification validation, and vendor review record.
