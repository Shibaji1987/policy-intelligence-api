# Employee Production Customer Data Access Standard

## Purpose

This standard controls employee access to production customer data for scheduled operations, reliability reviews, and approved maintenance.

## Policy

Employees may access production customer data for scheduled work when manager approval and system owner approval are recorded before the maintenance window. The approval record must include employee identity, maintenance ticket, production system, customer data category, access reason, and planned completion time.

Scheduled employee access may remain active for the approved maintenance window and must be reviewed within seven days. Employees with standing operational roles may use managed production credentials when the access is covered by a quarterly access review.

Employees must not copy production customer data into personal devices or unapproved analytics tools.

## Evidence

Required evidence includes manager approval, system owner approval, maintenance ticket, access review record, and audit log.
